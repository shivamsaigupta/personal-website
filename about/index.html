About

I'm mostly known for creating an internationally praised video-game called Terror Attack: Project Fateh. Most recently, I created an app called Tagmate. Its built using React-Native, Node.js and Firebase. It lets you discover and host events near you. I occasionally get invited to speak at conferences. I've spoken at a few TEDx conferences and given guest lectures at universities like National Institute of Design.
